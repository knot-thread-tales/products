Where It All Began

Knot & Thread Tales was born from a small corner in Hyderabad, where needles clicked and yarn unspooled into something beautiful. What started as a personal passion became a calling — to bring warmth into everyday moments through handcrafted work.

Every product we create carries a story. Whether it's a personalized gift bearing someone's name, a crochet toy that will be a child's first best friend, or a corporate hamper that leaves a lasting impression — we pour our heart into every stitch.

We believe handmade is not just a technique. It's a philosophy: of slowness, intention, and love made tangible.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Knot & Thread Tales, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you place an order via WhatsApp or our contact form, we collect your name, phone number, delivery address, and order details. This information is used solely to process and deliver your order.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send order updates and delivery notifications
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our products and services

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to deliver your order (e.g., sharing your address with a courier service).

Data Security

Your information is stored securely. We use Supabase, a secure cloud database, and follow industry-standard practices to protect your data.

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies or local storage to improve your browsing experience. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at knotthreadtales@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at knotthreadtales@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.

Last updated: January 2025

Products

All products are handmade. Minor variations in color, texture, or size may occur between the displayed image and the actual product — this is a natural characteristic of handmade goods and is not considered a defect.

Ordering

Orders are placed via WhatsApp. An order is confirmed only after you receive a confirmation message from us. We reserve the right to refuse or cancel orders if a product is out of stock.

Custom orders require full payment or a non-refundable advance before work begins. Once crafting has started, changes may not be possible.

We accept payment via UPI. Orders are dispatched only after payment is confirmed.
